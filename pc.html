Citadel Remonstered

About the Game

In the 24th century, the remote planet B104-GS12 hides a dark secret. Once a military outpost, then a penal colony, the site — known as the Citadel — became a covert laboratory for illegal biotechnological experiments on human subjects.

When all communication with the Citadel suddenly goes silent, a military spaceship is sent to investigate. Before answers can be found, the ship is destroyed, and you crash-land on the hostile world as the sole survivor.

Stranded 12 light-years from Earth, with no hope of rescue, you are forced to enter the Citadel's depths to complete your mission. To uncover the truth you must explore the mysterious halls, the remnants of forbidden experiments, and do what's necessary to ensure your survival.

It's you against the Citadel — and whatever horrors lurk within.

The game is a PC remake of the classic 1995 Amiga FPS game “Citadel”. It reuses the original assets and maps to provide a retro feeling, while adding modern gameplay and graphical enhancements.


More information

Citadel Universe is a dedicated website with all information about the game.


Where to play?

Citadel Remonstered is available for free from Good Old Games (GOG) for Windows and Linux.

There is also a web version which works in any modern browser (including mobile devices with a touch screen only) available via the Citadel Universe website.
