Battle Robots leaderboard update

5 May 2026

Battle Robots has been updated to periodically remove players who have been inactive for more than 6 months from the leaderboard, making it easier for active players to make progress. Any player can re-join the leaderboard when they become active again, they will then join at the end and will need to fight their way through the ranks.

Additionally, the leaderboard and active player stats are now available in the Battle Robots Leaderboad section. Note that the leaderboard on the website is synced with the game once per hour.
